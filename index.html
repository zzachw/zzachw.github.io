I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Jimeng Sun. Previously, I received my bachelor’s degree from Zhejiang University and the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign.

My research interest lies in developing deep learning algorithms that are generalizable and adaptable, with the aim of addressing significant real-world problems. I am particularly focused on healthcare applications, such as health predictive modeling, clinical natural language processing, and medical image analysis.
